## Mission & Philosophy

**Mission:** To assist athletes and active adults in regaining and maintaining healthy bodies aligned with their activity or sport demands through one-on-one personalized care blending rehabilitation and strength training.

**Approach:** "Reduce pain, educate, and get strong."

**Core Philosophy:**
- Evidence-based, total body approach
- One-on-one personalized care (no rehab aides)
- Client education on conditions, action plans, and self-management
- Emphasis on client empowerment and long-term independence from therapy
- "If you don't need me, then I have done my job"
- Strong bodies that move well are resilient

## Pricing & Payment

### Payment Model
- **Cash-based practice** - Does not accept traditional health insurance
- Accepts: Cash, credit card, HSA, FSA

### Insurance Reimbursement
- Out-of-network benefits may allow partial reimbursement
- Itemized CPT billing codes provided upon request
- Patient responsible for submitting paperwork to insurance
- Reimbursement rates vary and are not guaranteed

### Pricing Reference
- PT evaluation + 5 follow-up visits: $575-$650
- Payment plans available (ask)
- Package deals available (ask)
- Special promotions posted on Instagram (@matterapt)

### Visit Frequency
- Ideal: 1x/week (vs. 2-3x/week at insurance-based providers)
- Most non-surgical patients: 1-3 follow-up visits to become comfortable with self-management

### Limitations
- **Cannot treat Medicare or Medicaid patients** for covered PT services (legal restriction)
- Can accept payment from Medicare/Medicaid patients for non-covered services (e.g., strength training, maintenance tissue work)
